üìò Quick Glossary

  • Blinds: Forced bets that start the hand.
  • Position: Acting order. Later = more information.
  • Range: Set of hands a player could have.
  • C-Bet: Flop bet by the preflop aggressor.
  • Equity: Your share of the pot on average.
  • EV: Long-term profit of a decision.
  • Pot Odds: Price to continue vs. pot size.
  • GTO: Balanced, hard-to-exploit strategy.
  • Exploitative: Deviating to target opponent leaks.
