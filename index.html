🎮 Practice (Play)

Step-by-step to start playing now.

Free Practice Platforms

1) PokerNow.club

Private tables in your browser. No install.

  1. Open pokernow.club.
  2. Click Create New Room → set Players and Game.
  3. Share the table link with the team.
  4. Use Preferences and Video/Audio if needed.

Best for: Practicing real poker flow in a controlled environment.

2) Zynga Poker

Fast practice against many player types. Create a Zynga account or sign in with Apple/Google/Facebook.

  • Desktop: Install from Microsoft Store or play via web. Finish the tutorial, then join basic Hold’em tables.
  • Mobile: Install the iOS/Android app. Log in and play “Casual” tables.

Tip: Focus on fundamentals. Play-money results are irrelevant.

📘 Quick Glossary

  • Blinds: Forced bets that start the hand.
  • Position: Acting order. Later = more information.
  • Range: Set of hands a player could have.
  • C-Bet: Flop bet by the preflop aggressor.
  • Equity: Your share of the pot on average.
  • EV: Long-term profit of a decision.
  • Pot Odds: Price to continue vs. pot size.
  • GTO: Balanced, hard-to-exploit strategy.
  • Exploitative: Deviating to target opponent leaks.
