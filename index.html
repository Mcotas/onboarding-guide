ðŸ“˜ Quick Glossary

  • Blinds: Forced bets that start each hand.
  • Position: Acting order â€” later = more information.
  • Range: The possible hands a player could have.
  • C-Bet: Continuation bet from the preflop raiser.
  • Equity: Your average share of the pot.
  • EV: Long-term profit of a decision.
  • Pot Odds: Cost to call vs. pot size.
  • GTO: Balanced, hard-to-exploit strategy.
  • Exploitative: Adjusting strategy to target opponent mistakes.
